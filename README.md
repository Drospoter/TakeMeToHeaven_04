Name:
Take Me To Heaven.
Description:
A 3D platformer game where you have to make your way out of hell and reach heaven.
Genre:
Platformer 3D.
Victory condition:
Reach the Heaven's Gate.
Failure condition:
Lose all your hearts.

Custom Game Mode:
-The player starts with 3 hearts, each time they get hit by a skeleton or fall into the lava they lose a heart, there is no way to regain hearts and once you lose your last heart that's it for you.
-The player must reach 3 milestones, the golden pentagrams and the final destination, the heaven's gate, to win.
-The pentagrams are checkpoints for the player, if they die after reaching a checkpoint they will respawn back at the pentagram.
GameState:
The player starts with 3 hearts and is allowed to double jump.
UI:
The UI displays the player's number of hearts, if they die the heart they lost will appear as an inverted pentagram as a sign that it has been taken away.
Win Screen:
It depicts the main character, Beatriz,  reaching heaven from their point of view, there are clouds and the light is too bright.
Lose Screen:
It depicts Beatriz getting her head off for trying to escape hell as a punishment.
Mechanics:
The player must jump across various platforms and avoid the Hell bats that roam around, at the final stages the player must dodge the giant skulls with the double jump.
